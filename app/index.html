Helping patients take control of their long-term health

Digital Prevention Services Portfolio (DPSP) is part of NHS England. We’re here to help build the NHS that keeps you well, before you need the NHS that makes you better.

We play a central part in the government’s high-level strategy for the NHS:

analogue → digital
sickness → prevention
hospital → community

We build digital services, platforms and components to support NHS screenings, vaccinations, and personal prevention.

Our 10-year goal is radical reimagination of preventative healthcare.

  • Read our design histories for detailed notes on current work in progress

Current activity

Among other things, we’re currently working on:

  • Health Check online, a patient-facing service inside the NHS App
  • Manage your screening, a patient-facing service for checking screening history, and correcting any errors
  • Other staff-facing services to select cohorts, invite patients, and manage the operation of breast, bowel and cervical screenings
  • Manage vaccinations in schools, a service to help providers delivering school vaccinations
  • Manage your appointments a service for staff running vaccination clinics
  • Record a vaccination, a service to track which patient has been given which vaccination and when

For a full list, browse the prevention services catalogue, which includes services from DPSP and other organisations.
