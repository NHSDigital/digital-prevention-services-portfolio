Digital prevention services portfolio

Building the NHS that helps people stay well, before they need the NHS that makes them better

Digital Prevention Services Portfolio (DPSP) is part of NHS England. Our goal is radical reimagination of preventative healthcare: we're helping more people need NHS care less often. We're creating more opportunities for people to live healthier lives.

We play a central part in the government's high-level strategy to build an NHS that's fit for the future:

analogue → digital
sickness → prevention
hospital → community

We build and maintain the essential digital infrastructure for NHS preventative healthcare. We build practical digital tools for patients and NHS staff; and provide specialist expertise in user research, service design, content, technology, and more.

Current activity

We're currently focusing our efforts on:

  • screening for breast cancer, bowel cancer and cervical cancer
  • organising, managing and recording vaccinations for COVID-19, flu, RSV and pertussis, with teams devoted to infant, maternity and school vaccinations
  • personal digital prevention, primarily via the NHS App
